Know Us

Introduction:

The term hospice originated from the taverns of the Middle Ages where pilgrims on their way to the holy city of Jerusalem used to lodge. It started by providing medicine and food to the sick and the dying, as well as other needed help. The term hospice can be traced back to the latin word 'hospes' meaning that which contains both the host and the guest. Nowadays the hospice operates under the concept of giving loving care to terminally ill patients with incurable diseases along with their families. Thankot Hospice was established in 7th February 2007. This hospice takes care of terminally ill patients and their families with faith, hope and love in our Lord

While Managing The Thankot House


  1. Serves the terminal patients & their families taking care.....physically, mentally, socially & spiritually.
  2. Gives assistance at deathbed and at the funeral. Helping the patient to prepare for his/her death peacefully and fearlessly with faith & hope for the after life.
  3. Comforts and supports the bereaved family. Helping the bereaved to quickly overcome their sadness and return to their normal lives.
  4. Gives consultations on health & hospice education.
  5. Publishing the hospice magazine and other hospie related articles & documents.
  6. Spreads the hospice culture.
